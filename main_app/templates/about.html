About the Tech Collector

Tech Collector provides an easy way to log all of your prized pieces of technology.

Using the Tech Collector, you can log the different users of your tech and when they last used them.

You can also store the contact information for the users in case you need to reach out to them.

With the Tech Collector, you can see your usage habits and keep tabs on your tech!
